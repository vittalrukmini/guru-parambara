Guru Parambara — The Sacred Lineage

Brahmasri Vittaldas Maharaj was blessed to come under the grace of two great luminaries of the Bhakti tradition—Guruji Sri Haridhos Giri Swamigal and Paranur Mahatma Sri Sri Anna Krishna Premi Swamigal. As a young scholar deeply rooted in Vedic learning and devotion, he was drawn to the path of Nama Sankirtanam championed by Guruji. Under Guruji's compassionate guidance, he imbibed the ideals of Guru Bhakti, Nama Bhakti, and selfless service. 

Following Guruji's divine blessings, Vittaldas Maharaj came into the loving fold of Sri Sri Anna Krishna Premi Swamigal. Recognizing his devotion to Lord Panduranga and his dedication to spreading Nama Sankirtanam, Sri Anna affectionately bestowed upon him the title "Vittaldas." 

Thereafter, Maharaj continued to receive immense love, encouragement, and guidance from Sri Anna, carrying forward the message of Bhakti, Bhagavata Dharma, and Nama Sankirtanam. As a blessed disciple of both Guruji and Sri Anna, Vittaldas Maharaj stands today as a humble torchbearer of their spiritual legacy, inspiring devotees through discourses, bhajans, and dedicated service to Sanatana Dharma.

Sri Vittal Rukmini Samsthan, Govindapuram.
